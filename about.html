Biographical Story

A Life Dedicated to Scripture & Service

Pastor R. Naklee Quamele is a passionate gospel preacher, teacher, and trainer from Monrovia, Liberia. Called by God to proclaim the Good News with boldness and love, she has dedicated her life to ministry, discipleship, and equipping believers to live transformed lives for Christ.

Her ministry spans local congregations, regional conferences, and comprehensive discipleship training programs across Liberia and beyond. Through her preaching, teaching, and mentorship, she has impacted many lives, helping believers develop deeper faith, stronger biblical foundations, and the courage to live boldly for Christ.

With over a decade of ministry experience, she has trained leaders, empowered women in ministry, and established discipleship networks that continue to reach and transform communities.

Her Calling

A Vision for Gospel Transformation

Pastor Naklee believes every believer has the potential to become a powerful witness for Christ. Her vision is to see a generation of committed followers who understand Scripture deeply, live with integrity, and boldly proclaim the Gospel in their homes, churches, and communities.

Through preaching, teaching, mentorship, and strategic discipleship training, she works to develop leaders who will multiply her impact and extend God's kingdom across Liberia and beyond.
