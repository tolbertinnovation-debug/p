About the Minister

Called to Proclaim the Gospel

Pastor Naklee Quamele is a gospel preacher, teacher, and trainer from Monrovia, Liberia. Her ministry focuses on discipleship, leadership training, and equipping the local church to carry the Good News with boldness and love.
